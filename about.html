About Lux Search

Connecting exceptional talent with opportunities at industry-leading companies

Our Mission

Lux Search was founded in 2018 with a simple mission: to transform how professionals find meaningful career opportunities. We believe the traditional job search process is broken - too impersonal, too overwhelming, and too time-consuming.

Our curated approach focuses on quality over quantity. We partner with innovative companies to understand their unique culture and needs, then connect them with exceptional talent who will thrive in their environment.

With offices in New York, San Francisco, and London, we've helped thousands of professionals find fulfilling roles that align with their skills, values, and career aspirations.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We're committed to excellence in everything we do. From our curated job listings to our personalized service, we strive to exceed expectations at every step.

Human Connection

We believe in the power of human connection. Our team takes the time to understand both candidates and companies on a deeper level to create meaningful matches.

Integrity

We operate with unwavering integrity. Transparency, honesty, and ethical practices are at the foundation of every relationship we build.
